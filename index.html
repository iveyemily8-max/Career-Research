Digital Designer

By Emily Ivey

Career Description

A digital designer creates images, websites, and graphics for the internet. They use creativity and computer programs to make designs look good and easy to use.

Roles and Duties

  • Create designs for websites and apps
  • Make logos and graphics
  • Edit pictures
  • Work with clients or teams

Salary

Digital designers usually make about $50,000 to $85,000 per year.

Education Requirements

Most people need a degree in graphic design or digital design. Some learn through online classes and practice.

Required Skills

  • Creativity
  • Computer skills
  • Attention to detail
  • Good communication
